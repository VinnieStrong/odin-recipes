Omelette

French style omelette

with smoked ham and mature cheese

Ingridients:

  • 3 eggs
  • mature cheese
  • parmezan cheese
  • smoked ham
  • olive oil
  • mixed leaves salad

Steps:

  1. place the eggs in a bowl and mix them well
  2. place little bit of olive oil in a not sticking pan
  3. when the oil is really hot place the eggs
  4. when almost cooked place ham and cheese on top
  5. flip one half and cooke it for 1 min more on each side
  6. plate it w the salad and enjoy!!!
