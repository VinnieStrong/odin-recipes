Lasagna

The perfect Christmas beef lasagna

with slow cooked brisket, creamy mozzarella and parmezan cheese.

Ingridients:

  • fresh lasagna sheets
  • mozzarella
  • parmezan cheese
  • beef brisket
  • onion
  • carrot
  • celery
  • tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. cook half way the lasagna sheets in boiling salted water
  2. finely chop the onions, carrots and cellery and stir fried them until gold.
  3. place de brisket on top of the veggies and seer it an all sizes
  4. place the tomato sauce on top and cook for 2/3 hours and low/medium eat
  5. place the lasagna sheets in a flat oven proof container
  6. place the brisket/tomato sauce and add some parmezan
  7. place lasagna sheets on top and start over from point 6
  8. bake for 30/40 min and enjoy!!!
