Pan tomate

bread with grated tomatoes and extra virgin olive oil

Ingridients:

  • bread
  • tomatoes
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • salt

Steps:

  1. slice and toast the bread
  2. grate the tomatoes in a bowl trowing the skin away
  3. condiment with salt and olive oil
  4. place on top of he toasted bred and Enjoy!!
